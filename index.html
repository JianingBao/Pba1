Jianing Bao

Network is one of the most important things in Wellington's regular daily life. You can see many clues of internet by everywhere. This website will show you how internet get connected to our daily life.

Parking system

Parking system is one of the most common things for those people who live in Wellington. They helped government to manage the modern world, all the parking space are connected with terminal by internet, so when people pay for their parking, the system can connect them with bank and help manager to identify who paid for their parking space.

CCTV

CCTV also known as close-circuit television; this technology always used as a security device. There are many CCTVs in museum, bank, school, or other place that need to be protected. In Wellington, you will find numbers of camera on street, because CCTV can also help polices to catch illegal act such as overspeed or another criminal act. They will send their videos to cloud automatically though internet, and when the police or other people want to watch those videos, they can go to the cloud and download the video by connected to internet.
